Light on experience, or not sure where to start? Use AI Lab's **guided create** — it asks you questions like a chat and builds your first draft.

## How to use it

<Steps>
<Step title="Open AI Lab and say your intent">
Click **AI Assistant** to open [AI Lab](/en/guide/ai-lab), then say **"I want to create a resume from scratch, walk me through it"** (or click that suggestion).
</Step>
<Step title="Answer the questions">
It asks about your target role, experience, education, skills — **answer in plain words**, no formatting needed. Skip anything you can't answer yet.
</Step>
<Step title="Generate a draft → apply">
When you've talked enough, it says **"A resume draft has been generated"**. Click **Apply to resume** and the draft lands in your resume on the right.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The draft isn't the finish line. After it's generated, keep going — [tailor it to a job](/en/guide/ai-optimize), or refine it yourself in the [editor](/en/guide/editor).
</Tip>

<Note>
**Will this overwrite my content?** Guided create is for starting one from zero. If you already have a resume and just want to improve it, [Tailor to a job](/en/guide/ai-optimize) is the better fit — that proposes changes on top of your existing content.
</Note>
