When someone comments or replies on a resume you [shared](/en/guide/share-feedback), you're alerted in the **Notifications** center — no need to keep watching the link.

## Where to find it

Click **Notifications** in the left sidebar. It lists every alert, with **N unread** at the top.

Each notification tells you:

- **Who** (Someone / a specific user)
- What they did: **commented on** or **replied on** your resume

## What you can do

- **View details**: jump to the comment.
- **Mark as read**: clear the unread badge on ones you've seen.

<Note>
With no alerts it shows "No notifications yet". They appear here only when someone comments or replies on your resume.
</Note>

<Tip>
Notifications only matter alongside [share + cloud sync](/en/guide/share-feedback) — share a resume so others can comment, and the notifications will come.
</Tip>
