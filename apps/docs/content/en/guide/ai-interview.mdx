---

Before you send it out, practice a round yourself. **Mock interview** **reads your resume** and asks about the experience in it, back and forth like a real interview.

## How to use it

<Steps>
<Step title="Start a mock interview">
In [AI Lab](/en/guide/ai-lab), say **"Mock interview"** (or click that suggestion).
</Step>
<Step title="Answer the questions">
It asks about the projects/experience in your resume (e.g. "Which part of this project did you own? What was the biggest snag?"). **Answer as you would in a real interview.**
</Step>
<Step title="Read the feedback">
Based on your answers, it gives feedback, follow-up questions, and points out where the story is unclear — helping you sharpen how you tell it.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Pair mock interview with [deep analysis](/en/guide/ai-analyze): analysis tells you how well the resume **reads**, mock interview helps you practice how you **tell** it. When answering, lean on "context — action — result — number".
</Tip>

<Note>
⚠️ To verify visually: the exact mock-interview interaction (text-only Q&A / whether there's live voice) depends on the actual AI Lab UI.
</Note>
