**Magic Resume** helps you build a polished, professional resume fast: ready-made templates, AI that writes with you, and a live preview as you edit.

This **User Guide** assumes you're opening the app for the very first time and walks you through everything, step by step. **No technical background needed.** (Want to self-host or contribute? See the [Developer Handbook](/en/introduction) in the top nav.)

![Magic Resume — the main path](/images/user-journey.en.png)

## Three things to know first

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="No sign-up needed" icon="bolt">
    Start building right away — no account required. Your data lives in your own browser by default, visible only to you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sign in = sync everywhere" icon="cloud">
    Want the same resume on your phone and work laptop? Sign in and turn on cloud sync — see [Account & cloud](/en/guide/account).
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI is your assistant" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Polaris (北极星), the AI Lab assistant, helps you write, optimize, translate, and mock-interview — every change is proposed first and you can reject it.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
**What does "local mode" mean?** When you're not signed in, your resume lives only in this device's browser (nothing is uploaded). Switch browsers or clear your cache and it's gone. To back up safely and sync across devices, [sign in and enable cloud sync](/en/guide/account).
</Note>
