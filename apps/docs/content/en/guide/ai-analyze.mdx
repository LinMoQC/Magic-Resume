---
title: Deep analysis (resume health check)
description: Have three virtual interviewers score your resume — overall score, attribute matrix, strengths and gaps, and an optimization roadmap.
---

**Deep analysis** is like a "health check" for your resume: three interviewers with different perspectives read it and give a score plus concrete advice. Inspired by Lighthouse-style scoring.

## Three interviewers

It evaluates from three real hiring perspectives:

| Perspective | Cares about |
|---|---|
| **Peer Developer** | Technical detail, whether the approach is sound |
| **Tech Lead** | Impact, whether you can carry weight |
| **HRBP** | Culture, values, soft skills |

## How to use it

<Steps>
<Step title="Start the analysis">
In [AI Lab](/en/guide/ai-lab), say **"Analyze this resume's competitiveness"** (or click that suggestion).
</Step>
<Step title="Read the report">
After a moment you get a **Resume Analysis Report** with:

- **Overall Score**: the big-picture level at a glance.
- **Attribute Matrix**: scores across Key Skills Match, Impact & Actionability, Quantifiable Achievements, Clarity & Readability, Professional Summary, and more.
- **Strengths / Improvements**: concrete, itemized.
- **Optimization Roadmap**: what to fix first, then next.
</Step>
<Step title="Act on it, or export the report">
Follow the improvements back in the [editor](/en/guide/editor), or have AI [tailor to a job](/en/guide/ai-optimize). You can also **export the report**.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Each dimension is marked with a status (**Critical / Normal / Good**). Fix the "Critical" ones first — best bang for the buck.
</Tip>
