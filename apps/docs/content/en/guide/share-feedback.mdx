title: Share & comments
description: Generate a public link so others can drop comments right on your resume — and you can see and reply to them.
---

Want a mentor, friend, or recruiter to review your resume? Use **Share** to generate a link; they can **pin comments right on the resume**, and you'll be notified and can reply.

## Share it

<Steps>
<Step title="Click “Share”">
In the editor toolbar, click **Share** (sign-in required). This generates a **public link**.
</Step>
<Step title="Send the link">
Anyone with the link can open and view your resume. ⚠️ To verify visually: exact link permissions/toggles depend on the share panel.
</Step>
</Steps>

## How others leave comments

When a visitor opens the link:

- They switch to **Comment Mode** and **click anywhere on the resume** to add a comment (anonymous comments allowed, no sign-in needed).
- Comments are pinned to that spot and collected in a **comment list**.
- As the **Owner**, you see all comments and can **reply**.

## How you know someone commented

When someone comments or replies, you get an alert in the **Notifications** center — see [Notifications](/en/guide/notifications).

<Tip>
Get different people to look from different angles: a peer for technical detail, a lead for impact, HR for overall messaging — mirroring the three perspectives in [deep analysis](/en/guide/ai-analyze).
</Tip>
