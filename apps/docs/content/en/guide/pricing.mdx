description: What the free tier does, what paid adds, and where to upgrade.
---

Building resumes and exporting PDFs are **free** and need no account. **AI (AI Lab, AI PDF import) needs a signed-in account** — run it for free on **your own API key**, or subscribe to use platform credits (no key setup) and unlock more.

## Free vs paid

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free tier" icon="gift">
    The essentials: full editor, templates, export, plus a daily/weekly allowance of AI generations and available models.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paid tier (Pro)" icon="crown">
    Unlock everything: more (or unlimited) daily/weekly generations, more available models, the core model.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to upgrade

In the account menu, click **Upgrade to Pro** (or **Upgrade plan**), pick a tier, and complete the subscription. On success you'll see **Subscription created**. Your **remaining quota** is always visible in your account.

<Note>
**Tiers, prices, and features are configured in the backend and may change** — go by what the in-app upgrade page shows. Some capabilities may be marked "Coming soon".
</Note>

<Tip>
You can use AI without subscribing — **sign in**, then add **your own API key** in [Settings → Model Configuration](/en/guide/settings) (used by the backend instead of platform credits; the key stays in your browser).
</Tip>
