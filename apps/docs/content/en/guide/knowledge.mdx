Beyond building resumes, Magic Resume includes a **job-search knowledge base** — currently centered on the **Recruiting Timeline**, so you know "when to apply where".

## Recruiting Timeline

Click **Recruiting Timeline** in the left sidebar to see key dates for companies' campus / internship recruiting (applications opening, tests, interviews).

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the timeline">
Left sidebar → **Recruiting Timeline**. ⚠️ To verify visually: the exact company list, filters, and reminders depend on the actual page.
</Step>
<Step title="Plan your schedule around it">
See each company's application window clearly, and prepare the right version of your resume in advance (use [tailor to a job](/en/guide/ai-optimize) to tune each one).
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The timeline is **publicly viewable** — no sign-in needed. Use it like a calendar: circle your target companies' deadlines, then work backwards to schedule your resume prep.
</Tip>
