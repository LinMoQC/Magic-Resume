This page takes you **from zero to a resume you can send out**, in about 3 minutes. No account, no configuration.

<Note>
You only need one thing: open Magic Resume. For the official hosted version, go to [magic-resume.cn](https://magic-resume.cn); if you or a colleague self-hosted it, use that address.
</Note>

<Steps>
<Step title="Create a resume">
After the app opens you'll land on the dashboard (**Resumes** — the first item in the left sidebar). Click the **Create a new resume** card, type a name (e.g. "Jane-Frontend-2026"), and click **Create**.

<Tip>The name is just for you and you can change it anytime (click **Rename** on the resume card).</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in your content">
You're now in the editor. **The form is on the left, a live preview on the right** — every edit on the left updates the right instantly.

Start with **Basics** (name, contact), then work down through Experience, Education, Projects, Skills, and so on. Don't want to type it all? The next page shows how to [import an existing PDF/JSON](/en/guide/create-import), or you can let AI write it for you.
</Step>

<Step title="Pick a nice template">
Open the **Template** panel in the editor, choose one you like, and click **Change template** to apply it. To tweak fonts, colors, or single/two-column layout, click **Customize Template**.

<Tip>Changing templates never touches your content — only the look. Try them freely.</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Export a PDF">
When you're happy, click the export button to generate and download a PDF. ⚠️ To verify visually: the export button is usually at the top-right of the editor (near the preview). On success you'll see "Resume exported successfully".

Done! That PDF is ready to send.
</Step>
</Steps>
